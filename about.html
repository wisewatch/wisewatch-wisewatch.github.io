About Us

Show & Movie Finder is a modern web app designed to help you discover, track, and manage your favorite movies and TV shows. Whether you're looking for something new to watch, want to keep a watchlist, or track your viewing history, our app has you covered!

Features

  • Search for movies and TV shows with streaming availability and age ratings
  • User profiles with favorite genres, watch history, and profile photo upload
  • Watchlist and watch history management
  • Personalized recommendations and a discover page
  • Rate movies and get recommendations based on your taste
  • Progressive Web App (PWA) support for offline use

Technologies Used

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap
  • TMDb API for movie and TV data
  • LocalStorage for user data persistence
